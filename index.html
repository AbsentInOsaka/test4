"As the sun sets...

I can't stop myself from feeling excited...

An odd anticipation builds in my chest...

Soon after the evening began to turn that

shade of deep blue which reminds the people

returning home...

It began gradually.

where there had been only trees, or streetlights poking

above the sea, there was light.

The streelights shone without any practical purpose.

 

Now the lights shone for nothing but the sake of shining

These northern lights are all that is left of the old people."

 

Alpha, Yokohama Kaidashi Kikou, Ch. 22

Inspired by the Manga by Hitoshi Ashinano

Music: Melody of the Moon by Gontiti
